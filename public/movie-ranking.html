The Best Movies According to Kenn

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

This is my favorite movie of all time. The plot and the science are amazing. Matthew is a great actor

Inception

Leonardo DiCaprio is spectacular in this movie. The ending is always a good debate to have with others.

Pulp Fiction

I love Quentin Tarantino movies. He pushes the envelope and Uma Thurman was stunning in both Vol.1 and Vol.2.
